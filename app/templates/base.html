Product Pitches

A sales pitch is a well-crafted, then packaged sales presentation. Typically, a salesperson gets less than two minutes to explain how their business will benefit the prospect. In some contexts, it is also known as an elevator pitch since, with tight time constraints, you are presented with an opportunity to persuade a prospect to engage in business with you.

Promotion Pitches

While you might think the best way to get a promotion is to tout your strengths and say why you deserve a new title or bump in pay, that’s not always the case. Smart business owners don’t give promotions to help their employees, they promote employees to help the company. Create an effective pitch to move to the next level at your company by showing how the boss needs you.
